Graduate School of Information Sciences
Tohoku University, Japan

I am a postdoctoral fellow at GSIS, Tohoku University (Japan).
My main interest is algebraic combinatorics, primarily association schemes.
Currently, I work on higher-dimensional association schemes called association schemes on triples under Prof. Akihiro Munemasa.

Formerly, I was a postdoctoral fellow at SPMS, Nanyang Technological University (Singapore).
During that project, I investigated equiangular lines via association schemes with Dr. Gary Greaves and Dr. Jesse Lansdown.
